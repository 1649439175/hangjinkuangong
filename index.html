Gold Miner Classic

Dig Deep, Strike Rich!

Welcome to Gold Miner Classic, the legendary mining adventure that has captivated players worldwide! Use your mining claw to collect valuable treasures hidden beneath the earth.

How to Play

Click or tap to release the hook. Collect gold, diamonds, and other treasures. Watch out for rocks - they're worth less but weigh more!

Game Goals

Meet the target score within the time limit to advance to the next level. Upgrade your equipment between levels for better performance.

Pro Tips

Time your shots carefully! Bigger treasures are worth more points. Use power-ups wisely to maximize your score.
